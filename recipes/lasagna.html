Lasagna

A beautiful lasagna can be yours! All you have to do is follow the steps below. While this recipe will take some time and effort, it is so worth it!

Ingredients

  • 1 box of lasagna noodles
  • 1 jar of spaghetti sauce
  • 1 container of ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 1 package of ground meat
  • 1 bunch of parsley, chopped
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 1 green pepper, diced
  • 1 2lb package of shredded mozzarella cheese, divided into 3 equal portions
  • 1/2 cup of shredded parmesan cheese
  • 3 cloves of garlic
  • salt
  • pepper
  • olive oil
  • 9x13 pan
  • frying pan

Steps

  1. Cook the ground beef in the frying pan, adding salt and pepper to taste, onion, green pepper, and garlic about halfway through cooking. Cook beef until browned, then remove from heat and set aside.
  2. Cook noodles according to package directions. Drain and set aside.
  3. In a bowl, mix together the ricotta, egg, parmesan, and 3 tablespoons of the parsley.
  4. Add the jar of spaghetti sauce and 3 tablespoons of parsley to the beef and mix.
  5. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees.
  6. Grease the 9x13 pan, and pour a very light layer of meat sauce to the bottom of the pan. Top with a layer of lasagna noodles, a layer of sauce, and a portion of mozzarella cheese.
  7. Add another layer of noodles, top with the ricotta mixture.
  8. Continue in this order: noodles, sauce, 1 portion of mozzarella. Repeat.
  9. Bake for 40 minutes, or until bubbling and the mozzarella is turning brown.
  10. Remove from oven and let stand for 10 minutes.
  11. Top with chopped parsley before serving.
